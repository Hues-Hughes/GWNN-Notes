**Security+ START**

	CIA TRIAD (CAIN)
	
		Confidentiality
			Infor shoudl be only read by authorized persons
		
		Integrity
			Data is stored and transferred as intended and any modification is authorized
		
		Availablity
			Information is accessible to those authroized to view or modify it
			
		Non-Repudiation
			Persons cannot deny creation or modification of data
			
	
	Cybersecurity Frameworks
	
		NIST - National Institute of Science and Tech
			Identify
			Protect
			Detect  Respond  Recover
			
	Gap Analysis
		Tool to compare businesses current performance to desired future state.
		Performed by CISO designation. Upper Security Engineer, Manager, etc.
		
	Access Control (AAA/IAM)
		Umbrella Term for what tools can grant, limit access to areas within network, system, infra.
	
	
	Security Control Categories
	
		Managerial - Oversight
		Operational - Person for implementation/execution 
		Technical - Implemented in OS, software, security apps
		Physical - Devices that mediate access to premises and hardware
	
	Security Control Functional Types 
	
		Preventive - before attack. Restricts unauthorized access
		Detective - during attack. Identifies attempted or successful intrusions
		Corrective - after attack. Security policy and training updates.
		Directive - Rule Enforcement
		Deterrent - Psychological discouragements
		Compensating - Substitutes for principal control. manual processes, enhanced monitoring in trouble area
